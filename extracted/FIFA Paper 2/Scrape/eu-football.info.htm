 EUROPEAN NATIONAL FOOTBALL TEAMS   


The site is the complete archive of matches of all the European national football teams.

Here you can find full reports of all the matches played by national football team and recognized as official by national football association.

The site also contains statistics about all players, managers and referees who took part in international friendly football matches and tournaments.

Names of persons, clubs and venues are commonly given in their native languages. The Cyrillic names in languages of the former Yugoslav republics were translated into Serbo-Croatian, the non-Latin names in other languages were translated into English.

You can find additional information in users' comments with photos of national football teams, links to sources of information. Forum for our active users is also available.

If you liked our work and you want help us to make it better, please tell all your friends about this unique football database.
